Abstract

This project studies point cloud registration for thin, sheet-like cultural heritage artifacts. Such objects are challenging because their limited thickness and fragile geometry can produce ambiguous correspondences and unstable alignment. The proposed geometry-constrained bidirectional registration pipeline combines bidirectional matching cues with geometric restrictions to improve consistency and preserve surface structure during registration.
